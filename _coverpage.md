# Machine Learning Engineering Open Book

> An open collection of methodologies, tools and step by step instructions to help with successful training and fine-tuning of large language models and multi-modal models and their inference.

- Technical material suitable for LLM/VLM training engineers and operators
- Contains lots of scripts and copy-n-paste commands
- Brain dump of experiences training BLOOM-176B, IDEFICS-80B, and RAG models
- Continuously updated with new insights and techniques
